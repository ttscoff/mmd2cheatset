### Keyboard Shortcuts


#### Text shortcuts

Command|Description
--|--
^a|Beginning of line
^e|End of line
^f|Cursor forward
^b|Cursor back
^n|Next line
^p|Previous line
^k|Delete to end of line (kill)
^y|Paste buffer
